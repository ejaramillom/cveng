Personal Profile

Over the years 2016 - 2019 I developed and designed multiple cosmetic packages as well as their respective injection molds. Some of these products were lipsticks, powders, eyelashes and similar, working with interdisciplinary groups in environments with German technologies and staff. In administrative matters I have managed to implement methodologies for focus management (pomodoro technique), task management, kaizen methodologies, lean six sigma tools, 5's, chronograms, fishbone diagrams and other similar modern management approach tools.

In the period 2012-2016 I had the opportunity to make robots to automate kitchenware manufacturing lines (robots for making pots and pans), chair manufacturing machines, trenchers for planting flowers among others for different productive areas (lithography, stamping, food, recycling).

During the year 2019 I strengthened my skills in software development and industry 4.0, I developed landings for different brands, digital portfolio of plastic artists and various web structures in languages ​​such as JavaScript, frameworks such as ruby ​​on rails, React and Redux libraries, bootstrap and the like.
